Waterloop: Brake Component Design

Mechanical Trainee | 2025 Cycle

Overview

Waterloop is UWaterloo's SpaceX Hyperloop competition team. I served as a Mechanical Trainee during the 2025 development cycle.

Role & Responsibility

Specifically responsible for the design and manufacturing of secondary brake clamps and support brackets for the pod's deceleration system.

Technical Work

  • CAD: Developed 3D models in Onshape ensuring 0.05mm tolerances.
  • Material: Selected 6061-T6 Aluminum for strength-to-weight ratio.
  • Manufacturing: Hands-on operation of Lathe and Milling machine; precision carbon fiber cutting.

Results

The final bracket assembly achieved a 12% weight reduction compared to the previous iteration while maintaining structural integrity during 2g deceleration tests.
